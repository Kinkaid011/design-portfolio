About Me

I’m passionate about both psychology and visual design. I love understanding how people think and using that insight to create engaging experiences. My background as a biology student has sharpened my process-oriented mindset and strengthened my iterative approach to problem-solving. That mindset and approach helped me with web design clients and even in workshops with other designers. All of these experiences have made me a better and I have a lot of fun along the way. To me, great design isn’t just about how it looks—it’s about how it works and how it makes people feel.
